The Harry Potter series forges a world of magic and follows Harry Potter, a curently eleven year old boy living with his insolent aunt, uncle, and cousin. He is unexpectedley thrust into head first into a strange and foreign world where everyone seems to know him, as he is accepted into Hogwarts school of Witchcraft and Wizardry. While there, he learns more about the arcane arts and the mysterious death of his parents and his scar along with meeting his two best friends, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. As Harry uncovers his dark past, he is forced into battle with the most dangerous wizard in the world and constantly fighting his followers. The Harry Potter series see's Harry and his friends embark on epic adventures ,where both friends and enemies are made, all while attending Hogwarts, as the final showdown between him and the worlds most dangerous wizard draws closer.
The Harry Potter Series follows:
  • Harry Potter, the main protagonist, the story follows him and his fight with Voldemort for 7 years of his teenage life.

  • Ron Weasley, one of Harry's best friends, follows Harry on his adventures and has a passion for quidditch

  • Hermione Granger, Harry's other best friend follows Harry on his adventures, she is an extremely intelligent witch who excels at magic, though her parents are normal humans.

  • Draco Malfoy, one of harry's nemeses at school, he is the son of Lucius Malfoy and is from opposing houses. Harry suspects his family of conspiring with Voldemort.

  • Albus Dumbledore, the headmaster of Hogwarts and a mentor to Harry, Harry has much trust in him and considers Dumbledore a friend.
    •
  • Severus Snape, the potions teacher at hogwarts, he is bias towards Slytherin House and Harry believes that he is conspiring with Voldemort.
    •
  • Hagrid, a half man half giant who acts as the groundskeeper for Hogwarts, he is extremely close to Harry and Harry, along with Ron and Hermione, consider him a very good friend.
    •
  • Voldemort, the main antagonist, he is the most dangerous wizard in the world, with a grudge for Harry, he seeks to rule the wizarding world and enslave regular humans.
